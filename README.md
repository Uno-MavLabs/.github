# MavLabs Overview

MavLabs is an inclusive, collaborative community of UNO students dedicated to hands-on innovation, applied technology, and interdisciplinary projects.  

The organization:

- **Promotes student-driven exploration** of emerging technologies through team-based projects, workshops, outreach events, and partnerships with faculty, campus units, and Omaha’s broader tech community.
- **Upholds UNO’s values** of diversity, equity, access, and belonging across all programs and activities.
- **Supports entrepreneurial development**, helping members transform ideas into viable projects and, when appropriate, develop independent ventures or businesses that contribute to personal and professional growth.
